About AYUSH

The AYUSH sector encompasses traditional Indian systems of medicine including Ayurveda, Yoga & Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha, and Homoeopathy. This sector is dedicated to integrating these traditional practices with modern healthcare to promote holistic well-being.

The mission of the AYUSH Startup Registration Portal is to foster the growth and integration of traditional medicine systems in the global healthcare landscape. We provide a platform for startups in the AYUSH sector to register, gain support, and access resources to scale their businesses.

The Ministry of AYUSH is dedicated to the development of education, research, and propagation of indigenous alternative medicine systems in India. AYUSH stands for Ayurveda, Yoga & Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha, and Homoeopathy.

Ayurveda

Ayurveda, the science of life, is one of the oldest medical systems in the world. It emphasizes balance in the body, mind, and spirit, and uses natural herbs, diet, and therapies to maintain health and treat disease.

Yoga & Naturopathy

Yoga is a physical, mental, and spiritual practice that originated in ancient India. Naturopathy focuses on natural remedies and the body's vital ability to heal and maintain itself. Together, they promote holistic health.

Unani

The Unani system of medicine is based on the principles of the ancient Greek physician Hippocrates. It involves treating ailments by balancing the four humors: blood, phlegm, yellow bile, and black bile.

Siddha

Siddha is a traditional medicine system originating from South India. It is based on ancient texts and uses minerals, herbs, and lifestyle practices to prevent and treat illnesses.

Homeopathy

Homeopathy is a system of alternative medicine that uses highly diluted substances with the aim of triggering the body's natural healing response. It was developed in Germany in the late 18th century and is popular worldwide.
